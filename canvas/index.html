Start pin new data on IPFS with Deupload

Files uploaded to an IPFS bucket on Deupload are automatically pinned to IPFS and stored with 3x
replication across the Deupload infrastructure by default, at no extra cost to you.

Why pin your data on
IPFS with Deupload?
IPFS hpinning can be configured on locally hosted IPFS nodes, but for external, long-term storage, so that's why DeUpload exists as an IPFS pinning service.

With Deupload you get all the benefits of decentralized storage and other cutting-edge protocols with the frictionless experience you expect in a modern dev workflow. Check out our docs pages to learn more.
